Our History

MCS: Our History

We started 12 years ago under the name Lighthouse Technical Solutions Inc. and went under transforming changes during that time. Our motivations transformed from wanting to be service providers to becoming our client's partners. Our founder, Peter Caggiano, keeps the CEO position and has seen an increased capacity for scalability in the company, as well as a more solid trust from our clients.

MCS Technical Solutions is ready to face the challenges of the time, namely IA services, PaaS, IaaS and others, and we keep training and updating our staff in the latest technologies that your organization will require to grow.
